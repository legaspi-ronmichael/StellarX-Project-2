How It Works

1. Link Your Wallet

Paste your public address (starting with 'G') or select a simulation preset. This is completely secure: we only audit public ledger transaction records and never request private keys.

2. Audit History

Our algorithm automatically evaluates 4 financial markers: Account Longevity, Remittance Consistency, average cashflow volume, and Savings rate.

3. Secure Capital

Qualified OFWs (Grades A, B, or C) receive local business micro-loans from PHP 25,000 up to PHP 100,000 at low rates of 3% to 8% p.a.

Ledger audit is powered securely by Stellar Horizon.
